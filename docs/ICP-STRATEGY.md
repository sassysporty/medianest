# MediaNest — ICP Strategy & Implementation Guide

## Three Customer Segments

### 1. PRIMARY: The Overwhelmed Local Business Owner
- **Revenue**: $100K-$5M/yr | **Budget**: $500-$3K/mo
- **Industries**: Home services, health/wellness, food, professional services, retail, auto
- **Core need**: "I need more customers but have no time for marketing"
- **Entry service**: Free Audit → Website Creation or Local SEO
- **Upsell path**: Website → SEO → Social Media → YouTube

### 2. SECONDARY: The Ambitious Etsy Seller
- **Revenue**: $1K-$20K/mo on Etsy | **Budget**: $350-$1.5K/mo
- **Who**: Handmade/digital product sellers, mostly women 25-45
- **Core need**: "My products are great but nobody can find them"
- **Entry service**: Free Shop Audit → Etsy Listing Optimization
- **Upsell path**: Shop Audit → Etsy SEO → Social Media (Pinterest/IG)

### 3. TERTIARY: The Content-Curious Entrepreneur
- **Revenue**: $200K-$2M/yr | **Budget**: $1.5K-$7.5K/mo
- **Who**: Coaches, consultants, real estate, finance, fitness
- **Core need**: "I want YouTube authority but have zero time"
- **Entry service**: Free Channel Audit → YouTube Starter Package
- **Upsell path**: YouTube → Website → SEO

## Sales Process

1. **Attract** → Free Audit (lead magnet on every page)
2. **Capture** → Form submission → saved to Supabase → email notification via Resend
3. **Nurture** → Automated email sequence (3-5 emails over 7 days)
4. **Convert** → Strategy call via Cal.com booking
5. **Onboard** → Stripe payment → client portal access
6. **Retain** → Monthly reports, regular check-ins, upsell adjacent services
